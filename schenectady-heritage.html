About NY Capital Flippers LLC

Founded in 2018, NY Capital Flippers LLC is a premier real estate investment group specializing in the acquisition and rehabilitation of distressed residential properties across the Capital Region and Upstate New York. Backed by private equity, we manage a rolling portfolio of $5M+ in active developments. We partner exclusively with top-tier local contractors who value rapid payouts, clear scopes, and steady year-round project flow.

Our principal investment consortium has recently closed on a distressed heritage residential asset located within Schenectady, NY. The property has remained uninhabited for an extended lifecycle and necessitates a rigorous, code-compliant Full Gut Restoration to bring it up to modern market standards.

We are exclusively soliciting bids from licensed, bonded, and fully insured contracting firms or highly specialized architectural rehabilitation teams. The selected vendor must possess the logistical bandwidth to assume supreme operational control of this site immediately. To facilitate aggressive project pacing, all disbursements are structured strictly on a pre-funded milestone basis, ensuring uninterrupted liquidity and steady cash flow for the executing team.

Project Timeline & Execution Milestones

  • Phase 1 (Days 1-7): Mandatory Site securing, debris removal, and complete gut demolition.
  • Phase 2 (Days 8-21): Structural reinforcement, basement leveling, and weatherproofing shell.
  • Phase 3 (Days 22-35): Rough-in MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) and municipal inspections.
  • Phase 4 (Days 36-60): Drywall, interior finishes, fixtures, and final hand-over.
